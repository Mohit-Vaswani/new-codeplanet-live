FAQs

What is the CodePlanet’s Career Architect Program?
CodePlanet Career Architect Program is one of a kind Career Program designed to enhance your Technical skills, train you for your interview according to industry standards and get you placed in a company.

Is there a guarantee that I will get a Job?
Code Planet has always kept their promises and is a trusted brand in tech education with an experience of upskilling 5,000+ students with tech background. We will train you in a completely industry-oriented and do our best to help you land up in a tech job. From technical education to soft skills or code writing to resume building we will take care of each and everything.

What happens if I don't get a job?
In the rare case that you don't get a job, your entire payment including your registration fee will be refunded back to you.

Which roles will I be eligible for?
We are providing you training over multiple tech stacks so you will be eligible for multiple profiles like full stack developer, backend developer, devops Engineer, Software Engineer/Developer etc.
Which companies hire from Code Planet?
There are 100+ companies who have hired our students in the past including big tech giants and some renowned startups..

Do I need to submit any document post getting a job?
As per the ISA, you will be legally bound to furnish all the documents relevant to your Income such as Offer Letter, Salary Slips, Income Tax Returns, Bank Statements etc..

Do I need to deposit any kind of security collateral?
As we are going to invest in your learning but also we believe in our students to cover any risks. So you will have to sign an ISA with us so that our company, if the situation arises, can take appropriate legal action. For this purpose you will be required to submit photocopy of your government approved identity cards, blank cancelled cheque and photocopy of your college identity card.

How do I pay my ISA amount?
Our ISA payment collection is done through our NBFC (Non-Banking Financial Company) partners. When you get a job, as per the ISA conditions, our assigned NBFC partner will grant you a zero-interest capital equal to the ISA amount which you can pay in EMIs of 18 months.
